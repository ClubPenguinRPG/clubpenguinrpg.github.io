Wednesday, January 25, 2006

Game Testers Needed!



Hello Penguins!

As we are nearing the finish line on the new test server we are finally adding in some of the games. They may look the same on the outside, but they are brand new code on the inside that allows us to do some very cool things.

Some of you may have noticed one of the coolest new features that we've added, "watching!" That's right, now you can watch a game in progress and cheer on your friends! It will also help you learn the games if they are still new to you.

We have already begun to squash some bugs in the games, but we need your help to find them all. Please make bug reports as detailed as possible so we can squash them once and for all!

In other news: We are working on sledding right now hoping to have that launched in the next few days. After that we are finishing up the new board game.... wait... I've said to much already.

Until then... Waddle On!

Monday, January 16, 2006

We Need Your Help!

Hello Penguins!

We are happy to announce that the new test server has been launched! You can find it by clicking on the link under the Club Penguin login screen.

We know that there will be bugs and we have already begun working on them. But please help us by reporting any bugs you find in as much detail as possible including the room you were in and what you were doing when the bug happened. If you are able to make it happen over and over please describe how you do it.

Many of you will notice a speed difference and you should because this is a big update to the code. Thanks for all your patience and help!

The faster we can find and fix all the bugs, the faster we can begin releasing new games.

Monday, January 09, 2006

Where is Rsnail?

Hello Penguins!

Many of you have been wondering why you haven't seen Rsnail online in a while. We thought we would let you in on a little behind the scenes info of what he's been working on.

Other than working on new games and new art, Rsnail has been hard at work late into the night on the last and final "rebuild" of the Club Penguin program code. Our goal is to make Club Penguin as fun and fast as possible. Rather than just keep working on little pieces of it here and there, we decided to dive in and make the whole thing as fast as possible.

We're nearing the final stages of this process and this means we will probably launch a test server again. Yes, this also means we will give away a few clothing items to those who help us test on the server, but we will talk more about that later.

Anyway, once this is done, we will be able to focus all of our energy on making even more games, rooms, and other great additions to Club Penguin.

We just wanted to keep all you Penguins up to date...

Until then... Waddle On!
