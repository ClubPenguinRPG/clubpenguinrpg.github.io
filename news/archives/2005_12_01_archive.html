Tuesday, December 20, 2005

Two Big Changes!

Hello Penguins!

Two big changes today!

First, we have updated the igloos to allow them to be shown on the map. We did this because people were using the buddy list to see people's igloos and not because they were really friends. If you were one of these people, please clean up your buddy list and use the map from now on to check out igloos. If you want to make your igloo public, go to your igloo and click on the "lock" picture to make it open or closed to other penguins. Your buddy list will still be able to go there anytime. Note: It will only appear on the list when you are logged in.

Second, we have had to change the focus of this blog because the comments were getting too off topic and it was taking too long to go through the hundreds of comments each day. That meant less time for working on Club Penguin. So from now on this area is going to be used for development news only. That means we will post any updates that we are making to Club Penguin and will continue to post your comments about those updates. Any comments that are not related to the update won't be posted. Please continue to submit your ideas, what you like about the updates and what you don't like so we can make Club Penguin better. Please don't submit the names of your buddies that you are mad at this week. I hope this makes sense.

If you are looking for the latest Club Penguin news, please read the "in game" newspaper that comes out every Thursday. We will soon be delivering daily updates in game as well.

Thanks and Waddle On!
-Club Penguin Team
