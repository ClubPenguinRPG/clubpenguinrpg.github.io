Monday, January 16, 2006

We Need Your Help!

Hello Penguins!

We are happy to announce that the new test server has been launched! You can find it by clicking on the link under the Club Penguin login screen.

We know that there will be bugs and we have already begun working on them. But please help us by reporting any bugs you find in as much detail as possible including the room you were in and what you were doing when the bug happened. If you are able to make it happen over and over please describe how you do it.

Many of you will notice a speed difference and you should because this is a big update to the code. Thanks for all your patience and help!

The faster we can find and fix all the bugs, the faster we can begin releasing new games.

For helping us, we have hidden one clothing item in the test server that both members and non-members can have! Good luck finding it!

Until then... Waddle On!

- Club Penguin Team
