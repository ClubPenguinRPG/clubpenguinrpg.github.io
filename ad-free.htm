Club Penguin is an ad-free zone.

At Club Penguin we're passionate about creating an online entertainment experience that provides fun for children and peace of mind for parents.


We value the trust parents place in us by allowing their kids to contribute to, and participate in, the fun, imaginative place that is Club Penguin. It is a big responsibility and one we do not take lightly.


Although most websites rely on advertising to fund their operations, we feel the young age of our users demands a different approach. Because we want Club Penguin to be a place filled with good, clean fun where the focus is about playing games and waddling around with friends we maintain a firm policy prohibiting third party advertising.
Thanks to the ongoing support of those who purchase subscription memberships we are able to maintain that ad-free status, which we believe benefits every child who plays Club Penguin.


We are proud of our unwavering commitment to keeping Club Penguin free of third party advertising. If you have any questions or require further information contact our Community Support team at support@clubpenguin.com
