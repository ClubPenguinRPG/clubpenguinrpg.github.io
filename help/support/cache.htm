Clear your Cache
Your browser may be caching an old version of the game. To fix this and load the current version of Club Penguin, try clearing your web-cache.


To do this in Internet-Explorer:

  1. Go to the ''tools'' menu at the top web broswer
  2. Select ''Internet Options''
  3. Click to delete temporary internet files

In Firefox:

  1. Go to the 'tools' menu at the top web broswer
  2. Select 'options'
  3. Select 'privacy'
  4. Click the Clear button for cache
