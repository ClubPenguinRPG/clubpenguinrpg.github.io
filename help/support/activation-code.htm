Find My Activation Code

To resend your activation code, follow these steps:

1) Click on the link below. If nothing happens when you click on the link (or if you use AOL), copy and paste the link into the address bar of your web browser.

http://support.clubpenguin.com/lost-code.php

2) The link will take you to our Lost Activation page. Fill in the appropriate fields to resend your activation code.

Note: Be sure emails from support@clubpenguin.com are not blocked!

Some email providers may automatically put emails from us into your ''junk'' or ''bulk'' mail folder. Please check there if you do not see the email in your Inbox.

If you cannot find your activation code, try creating another penguin.
