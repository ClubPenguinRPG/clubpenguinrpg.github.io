Become a Member

Becoming a member of Club Penguin is easy. Follow these three steps:

  1. Create a penguin.
    To do this, select the "Create an Account" link at play.clubpenguin.com and follow the instructions.*

  2. Choose your membership package.
    Once your penguin account is activated, visit our Membership page and select a membership package.

  3. Confirm your membership.
    Check your email to find your membership receipt confirming your purchase. The next time you login to play, your membership will be waiting for you.

 

*Be sure to activate the account as instructed in step 1 prior to purchasing a membership.
