I’m not sure what kind of computer I should be using?

Club Penguin will work best on computers with these settings:

If you use a PC, it should have:

Processor: Pentium III
Speed: 500MHz
Modem Speed: 56K modem or better
Operating System: Windows 98, NT, 2000, ME, XP, or Vista
Web Browser: Internet Explorer 6, Netscape 7.2, or Firefox 1
Flash Player: Adobe Flash Player 9

Every type of PC is different, so you may have to ask an adult to help you check the settings. If you use WinXP you can right-click on “My Computer” on the top of your screen. Then click on “Properties” to get most of the information.

 

If you use a Mac, it should have:

Processor: PowerPC G3, G4, G5, or Intel core
Modem speed: 56K or better
Memory: 256MB RAM
Operating System: Mac OS X 10.3
Web Browser: Firefox 1.0.3 or better, Safari 2.0.4

Flash Player: Adobe Flash Player 9

To check your settings on a Mac, click the blue apple on top of your screen. Then click on “About This Mac” to get most of the information.
