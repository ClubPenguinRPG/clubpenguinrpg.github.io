I need to know how fast my connection should be?

Club Penguin is designed to work with a 56 K modem or higher. Most computers with a dial-up Internet connection have a 56 K modem. This will work, but you may find that it takes a long time to load things or connect.

These Internet connections are faster:

  1. Use a modem with a speed of 2MB per second with your dial-up connection.

  2. Use a computer with a DSL connection.

  3. Use a computer with a cable connection.

  4. Use a computer with a fiber-optic connection.
