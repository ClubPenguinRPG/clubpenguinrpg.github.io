Open Your Igloo

  • If you are a member, you can open your igloo to other players. This means they can come and visit you, even if they are not on your buddy list.
  • When you are in your igloo, if you would like other players to visit you, click on the lock icon on the right side. This will “open” your igloo to other players by adding it to the map.
  • If you want to visit another player’s igloo, open the map, click on the igloo, and select the name of a penguin who you would like to visit.
  • To close your igloo, click the lock again.
  • You can always visit a buddy’s igloo, even when they are not online. Simply open your buddy list, select their name to bring up the player card, and then click on the house icon.

Edit Your Igloo (Members Only)

  • Every player gets an igloo to call home. To go to your igloo, click the house icon on the right of your toolbar.
  • Once in your igloo, you can buy and move furniture. To buy new igloo furnishings or move items you already own, you must be in EDIT mode. (Note: You must be a member to purchase items).
  • To get to EDIT mode, click on the tape measure on the bottom right.
  • Once in edit mode, you can click the catalogue on the right of the screen to buy items for your igloo.
  • To see the items you own, click on the box above the catalogue. This will show you what you have in storage.
  • When you buy a new item, it automatically goes into storage.
  • To move items from storage into your igloo, click the item, then click where you want to place it.
  • Items in the igloo can be moved by clicking the item, and then clicking where you want to place it.
  • To rotate an item, click the item, then use your Left and Right arrow keys on your keyboard. The up and down keys will alter some items in a different way, for instance, the up arrow will fill the garbage can, while the down arrow will empty it. Use Up and Down arrows with different items to see what they do.
  • To move an item from the igloo into storage, click the item, then click out of the igloo and it will automatically go back into storage.
  • To buy an item from the magazine, first click the magazine to open it. Then click the item you wish to buy, and click ‘yes’ if you wish to buy it. It will be added to your items in storage, and the coins will be removed from your account.
  • IMPORTANT: You must get out of edit mode in order to exit your igloo or move around, and to save your changes. To get out of edit mode, click the tape measure again.
  • Use the map to exit your igloo. If you walk to the door, the map will open automatically.
