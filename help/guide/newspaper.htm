Newspaper
  • Every week there is a new edition of The Penguin Times newspaper. When a new paper has been released, you will see the word ‘New’ on top of the newspaper. You can read the paper by clicking on the icon at the top left of your screen.
  • You can see when another penguin is reading the paper.
  • You cannot talk to other people while reading the paper, and if you talk to someone who is reading the paper, they will not see what you said.
  • If you would like to submit a poem, riddle, joke, or comic strip to the newspaper, open the paper and click on “We need you!” in the top left corner. Then follow the instructions there.
  • To view old newspaper editions go to the Boiler Room and click the top drawer of the filing cabinet. The Penguin Times keeps six previous publications available at a time.
