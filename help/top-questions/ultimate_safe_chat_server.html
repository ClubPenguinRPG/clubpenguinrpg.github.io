What is an Ultimate Safe Chat Server? 

There are two different types of servers in Club Penguin. ‘Ultimate Safe Chat’ is one, and is indicated with a little speech bubble beside each server name on the login page.

In these servers you communicate with other penguins only by using quick phrases and emoticons located on the bottom action bar. Users who log onto these servers cannot type their own messages, regardless of their account chat settings.
