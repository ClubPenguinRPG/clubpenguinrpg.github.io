Walk Around

Club Penguin is connected by a series of pathways. To move from one location to another, use your mouse and click the area you want to move to. There is a lot to explore. Click on doors, stairs, signs and paths to discover where they lead.
back to top

Map

The map is another way to move from one room to another. It is located on the bottom left corner of your screen. When you open the map, click an icon to visit that place. There are few places in Club Penguin that are only accessed by the map.
