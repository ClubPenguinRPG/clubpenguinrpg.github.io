When will my order arrive?

The amount of time it takes your order to arrive at your home or post office box depends on the shipping method you choose. Please visit our Shipping Information page for more information on estimated arrival times.

back to top

How can I tell if my order was successfully processed?

After you enter your credit card information and click “Submit Order,” a window will display the order number and the list of items ordered. An order receipt will be sent to the email address you provided with your billing information.

back to top

When will my order ship?

Orders are usually shipped from our distribution facility within one to two business days of the date the order was placed.

back to top

How can I track my order?

If you chose one of the UPS shipping options, a tracking number is supplied in the Shipment Confirmation email you were sent. You can use this number at UPS.com to track your order status. This option is not available for orders shipping through the United States Postal Service (USPS).

back to top

How can I see what my order will cost, before I pay for it?

When you checkout your order, enter your address, and click "Continue", an itemized receipt will be displayed with the total cost of the order, including shipping and handling. On this page you will have the opportunity to make any changes you would like. It is not until you click "Place Your Order", enter your credit card information, and click "Submit Order" that the order is placed and your credit card is charged.

back to top

There is an error in my address on the order I just placed. Can I change it?

Because orders are processed at our distribution facility within 24 hours of order placement, it is not always possible to make an address change. If you need to change the address on your order, please email us as soon as possible at .

Can I make a change to my order after it has been placed?

Order changes are possible but not guaranteed. The Club Penguin Shop must be contacted within 24 hours from when the order was placed to make any changes. Send your request to While we will do our best to meet your request, we cannot guarantee it. All changes must be intercepted before distribution processing has occurred.

back to top

An item in my order is defective/doesn’t fit. How do I exchange it?

We would be happy to replace an item in your order if it is defective or doesn’t fit. Please see our Customer Service page to find out how you can get an exchange or a refund.

back to top

I need to return something for an exchange/refund, but I've lost the original packing slip. What do I do?

Please write your name, address, and order number on a piece of paper and include it with your return. Also, please write on the paper the item you are returning, and what you would like to receive in exchange for it. For more information on returns and exchanges, please see our Customor Service page.

back to top

How do I know what size clothing item to order?

Each clothing item has a product page with a link to its corresponding sizing information. Please read it over carefully before making your purchase. If you are still unsure after reading the sizing information, compare the dimensions of a shirt you own that fits well to the dimensions given for the shirt you want to purchase from our shop. Our dimensions show the width measured under the armpit, and the length measured down the center of the back.
back to top

Can I pay for an order without a Credit Card?

Currently, you can use Visa or MasterCard to pay for your Club Penguin merchandise. We do not accept cash, checks or money orders at this time.

back to top

Can I pay with a currency other than US Dollars?

The Club Penguin Shop accepts all forms of currency that can be paid through Visa or MasterCard. All currencies are automatically converted to US funds by our payment processing company.

back to top

When should I place an order to receive it by a certain date?

If you need to receive your order by a certain date, we strongly recommend you read over the available shipping methods and estimated delivery times on our Shipping Information page.
