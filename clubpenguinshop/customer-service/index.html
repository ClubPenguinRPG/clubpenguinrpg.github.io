Hours of Operation:

Emails are answered during regular business hours, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Pacific Standard Time, Monday through Friday. Our distribution facility ships orders from 7:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Pacific Standard Time, Monday through Friday.

Method of Payment:

The Club Penguin Shop accepts Visa and Mastercard.

Returns & Exchanges:

If you receive a product that is in any way defective, the wrong size, or the wrong color from what you ordered, please send it back in the condition you received it for return or exchange to:

Club Penguin Shop Distribution Facility
4 South Idaho Street
Seattle, WA
98134-1119

Please include the original packing slip that came with your shipment, along with your name and complete address, in the original packaging. Returns and exchanges must be made within 30 days of receiving your order.

For a return, upon inspection of the merchandise, we will refund the credit card used for the original purchase for the total amount of the merchandise. We are unable to refund any shipping and handling charges incurred.

In the case of an exchange, please write on the original packing slip the item you would like to exchange it for, along with size, color, etc. We are unable to refund any shipping and handling charges incurred.
