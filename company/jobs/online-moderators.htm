Community Service Representatives

Club Penguin is looking for self-motivated, enthusiastic individuals to join our support team.

  • Be able to work well in a team environment.
  • Must be 18 years of age or older.

Salary:

  • Competitive wage and benefits based on experience and qualifications.

Location:

  • Must be able to work in Kelowna, BC, Canada.
