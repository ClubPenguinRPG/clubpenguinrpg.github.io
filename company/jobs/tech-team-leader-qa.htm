Technology Team Leader, Quality Assurance

Organize Quality Assurance (QA) tasks and priorities for the QA testers for all virtual world features, tools and web functionality in order to ensure a safe, consistent and positive experience for the audience online across all cultures globally.

 

Responsibilities:


Oversee and schedule QA testing workflow and prioritizing tasks, reporting regular team progress updates to QA Manager and all appropriate internal stakeholders.

Support team members in their daily tasks; communicate priorities as set out by the Manager and Production schedule.

Review all bug submissions and liaisons with Production and Programming teams to expedite fixes.

Document and standardize QA process, methodologies and reporting, ensuring they are followed by the QA test teams globally.

Develop, implement and maintain comprehensive test plans and wiki updates to ensure quality standards and processes within the organization, locally and internationally.

Work with Production and Technology teams on understanding of the feature scope and development schedule.

Provide and coordinate QA training and mentoring for team members (will include initial training of new hires locally and globally QA).

Maintain long term forecasts of projects with resource availability as a focus.

Creating and maintain weekly Risk Registers on any changing timelines or scope that could have an impact on the QA schedule.

 

Qualifications:


Post-Secondary Certificate or Degree in Computer Science or equivalent.

Project Management Certificate or equivalent experience preferred.

5+ years leadership or management experience in a QA and/or Technology department.

5+ years QA experience with a focus on game products.

3+ years of web content and application QA experience preferred.

Experience with localization testing strategies for complex languages.

Experience with testing mass user website/games including smoke, integration stress, performance/load testing using custom scripts and some automated tools.

Formal training and experience with QA tool, processes and methodologies.

Proficiency with Microsoft Office including MS Excel, MS Project, MS Outlook, and MS Visio.

Previous experience with MS Project, JIRA bug database and internal wiki/web portal update experience preferred.

Experience on multiple platforms, both client and server (Windows, Mac, Linux).

Proficient with any of the following an asset: Flash, XML, SQL, ActionScript, JavaScript, UI, CSS and layouts, Web 2.0, and be prepared to make recommendations on additional toolsets.

Fluent in English.

 

Skills and Abilities:

 

Passion for QA, online gaming and children.

Strong supervisory skills.

Strong conflict resolution skills.

Able to work independently and take initiative.

Creative and resourceful when it comes to problem solving, devising creative ways of testing effectively, improving team processes and efficiency.

Excellent knowledge of Web technologies.

Knowledge of online games and an interest in Massively Multi-Player Games.

In depth knowledge of production and technical development project lifecycles.

Able to multitask and prioritize heavy workload under pressure.

Able to retain and manage detailed project information.

Understands how to work with remotely located teams.

Able to define or redefine processes if existing processes are inefficient or need to be created.

High attention to detail.

Collaborative and positive working style.

Proven knowledge of executing and reporting on multiple projects within a tight timeline.

Understanding of Website production.

Able to manage relationships with internal stakeholders, including Creative, Production and Technology Management and developers.

Able to respond to project demands with creativity and enthusiasm.

Able to clearly articulate suggestions and ideas in both written and verbal form.

Able to collaborate, build and organize teams; assigning and monitoring tasks.

 

Salary:

 

Competitive wage and benefits based on experience and qualifications.

 

Location:

 

Must be able to work in Kelowna, BC, Canada
