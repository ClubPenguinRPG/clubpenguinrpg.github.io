United Kingdom

Club Penguin, the fastest growing virtual world where kids gather to play games, interact with friends and let their imaginations soar is expanding its borders to the UK.

We are looking for individuals who share our commitment to making a safe and fun online entertainment experience for kids. Those who value community, have a sense of fun, and a commitment to keeping kids safe online are needed!

To join our team, you must demonstrate a passion for providing excellent customer care for kids and their families. Basic computer skills are required.

Positions Available:

  • Player Support Representatives
