Director of International Development

Carry out Club Penguin mandate to expand the virtual world into international markets.

 

Responsibilities:

 

Develop and implement system of communication between localized (abroad) offices and main office (Kelowna).

Immediate report for managers of local Club Penguin offices abroad. Provides support and resource for these teams.

Ensure brand promise is protected and met in all development abroad.

Primary contact between Club Penguin and DIG on all aspects of international development.

Oversees implementation and execution of strategic roll out of international development in its various locations in conjunction with DIG International teams in these places.

 

Qualifications:

 

Bachelor degree in Business or other related field

Minimum 6-8 years managing teams and launching projects or products in International markets

English, plus at least one of the following: French, Portuguese, Spanish, Japanese, Mandarin

 

Skills and Abilities:

 

Can travel; flexible schedule

Highly motivated; implement and develop position where it does not currently exist; directorial

Turns the conceptual into the tangible

Creative approach to challenges; innovative problem solver

Proven values that are in line with Club Penguin’s approach to children’s entertainment

Articulate; strong communicator across cultural and institutional variance

Deeply passionate about kids and online play

A decision-maker; leads with vision and objectives that are clearly outlined and articulated to those being led.

Highly relational: cultivates strong and positive lines of communication

Proven project management skills at, or equivalent to an international level.

 

Salary:

 

Competitive wage and benefits based on experience and qualifications.

 

Location:

 

Must be able to work in Kelowna, BC, Canada

Club Penguin will assist with relocation
