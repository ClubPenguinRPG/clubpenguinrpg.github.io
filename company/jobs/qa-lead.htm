Team Leader, Quality Assurance Game Testing

The Team Leader, Quality Assurance Game Testing will organize and oversee a team that tests all online content, games and new applications in order to ensure a safe, consistent and positive experience for the audience online and the staff using the tools.

 

Responsibilities:

 

Ability to oversee and schedule QA testing workflow and prioritizing tasks, reporting regular team progress updates to QA Manager & all appropriate internal stakeholders.

Coordinate with the QA Manager to create a testing schedule that meets the needs of each department.

Develop, implement and maintain comprehensive test plans and wiki updates to ensure quality standards and processes within the organization, locally and internationally.

Work with Production Teams on understanding of the feature scope and development schedule.

Provide and coordinate QA training and mentoring for team members. (Will include initial training of QA Game Testers from International offices)

Working and collaborating closely with Build Engineer to ensure assets are up and available for testing, based on Production/testing schedule timelines.

Able to provide testing support to the team when bigger features require the testing coverage.

 

Qualifications:

 

Post-secondary certificate or degree

Project Management experience a plus

3-5 years of previous industry experience

Minimum of 2 years of relevant QA experience with at least 1 year in a leadership or management role

Software skills: Microsoft Outlook, MS Word, MS Excel, and basic Apple/Mac skills

Fluent in English

 

Skills and Abilities:

 

An excellent understanding of the English language

Excellent communication skills both written and spoken

Proven knowledge and experience in QA disciplines and testing structures in the game industry

Excellent ability to define or redefine processes if existing processes are inefficient or need to be created

Organized and efficient time management skills

Experience with working to deadlines

Passion for online gaming
