International HR Generalist

The HR Generalist, International will coordinate with international offices for all HR issues, including hiring efforts, HR practices and policies, and employee support.

 

Responsibilities:

 

Support international offices in all HR issues.

Develop, document, communicate, and implement HR policies for all international offices.

Partake in the hiring process for the international offices; coordinate between local Head of CP, Director of HR (Kelowna), VP of Operations (Kelowna), and Disney HR.

Research, understand, document, and communicate the legal HR requirements of each international office.

Research HR issues including legal requirements, local workplace trends, markets, and resources, in areas CP is considering for opening future branches.

Ensure the Director of HR, International Development Project Manager, and VP of Operations are always aware of the status of international HR issues.

Liaise between the international offices and Club Penguin and Disney staff on all HR issues.

All other miscellaneous tasks related to supporting international HR needs.

 

Qualifications:

 

Relevant college or university degree

+3 years Human Resources experience

Fluent in English, additional languages a bonus

 

Skills and Abilities:

 

Able to prioritize

Excellent communicator (written and verbal without the benefit of body language)

Flexible and open minded

Organized

Able to give direction in a clear manner

“People first” personality

Knowledge of different cultures and open to learning new ones

Detail oriented

Able to multi-task

 

Particulars of the Position:

 

Travel may be required. Must be able to travel internationally. Non-standard business hours may be required to ensure prompt communications with other time zones.

 

Salary:

 

Competitive wage and benefits based on experience and qualifications

 

Location:

 

Must be able to work in Kelowna, BC, Canada
