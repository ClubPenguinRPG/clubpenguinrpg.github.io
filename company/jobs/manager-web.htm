Manager of Web Design

Club Penguin, a part of the Walt Disney Company, is looking to expand its growing team of skilled, conscientious employees who are needed for a fast growing online game for kids. We are a community-oriented company with a fun and dynamic workplace located in Kelowna, BC, Canada.

Summary:

This key design management position will help drive strong brand awareness, keeping the Club Penguin web site in world-class form.  This person is passionate about people; team members (as a manager) and end users of the web site. On top of being a strong manager and clear communicator, he/she is capable of implementing excellent user-centered design, information architecture, and interactivity.

Responsibilities:

  • Motivating and managing the web team, as well as coordinating production schedules
  • Art direction and oversight of usability of all present and future web sites
  • Develop and prototype new features, functions, and interfaces to meet brand and user requirements
  • Creation of creative briefs and style guides
  • Ensure quality and continuity of work
  • Gathering, reviewing and validating project requirements

Qualifications:

  • Diploma/degree in graphic arts and/or a formal fine arts degree
  • Strong design portfolio
  • 6-7 years working experience in web and interactive design, including at least two years experience in art direction and staff management
  • Extensive understanding of graphic design for Internet applications, including accessibility for the web
  • Excellent knowledge of HTML, XML and XHTML, Flash, JavaScript and CSS
  • Good knowledge of PHP, MS-SQL and MySQL  
  • Expert in web authoring tools such as Dreamweaver, InDesign and Photoshop
  • English language – other languages a plus

Skills and Abilities:

  • Strong interpersonal skills, with ability to bring out the best in a creative team
  • High proficiency with cross-browser and cross-platform issues
  • An understanding of technical constraints of web technology
  • Self-assuredness that allows you to stand up for your work while being open to constructive criticism.
  • First-rate resourcefulness
  • Vast knowledge of creating and designing user interfaces
  • Excellent communication skills including ability to articulate creative concepts and rationale for client and team
  • Comprehensive understanding of interactivity and the Web
  • Ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously
  • Motivation, decisiveness, organizational skills, efficiency, and dedication
  • Ability to think and respond quickly maintaining attention to detail
  • Experience with creative brainstorming, campaign development, creative brief development, and creative execution

Salary:

  • Competitive wage based on experience and qualifications.

Location:

  • Must be able to work in Kelowna, BC, Canada
