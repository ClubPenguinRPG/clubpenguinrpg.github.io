Director of Finance and Accounting

Position Summary:

  • Full responsibility of Club Penguin's operational finance and accounting including but not limited to designing, implementing, and administering the processes and infrastructure needed to successfully support and continue building the business.

  • Operational responsibility includes, but is not limited to, administering internal financial controls, working with all levels of the organization (Senior Management and lower) to communicate financial results and financial processes. Periodically interface with internal and external governing bodies (e.g. Corporate Controllership, Corporate Tax, PricewaterhouseCoopers, etc.), administering policies & procedures, and managing a small team (initially three direct reports) that will effectively support all financial aspects and financial controls of the business.

  • Full service FP&A responsibility which includes but is not limited to building and maintaining bottom-up and top-down models that track and forecast financial results (P&L, Headcount, Capital Spending) as well as key metrics of the business month-by-month (or week-by-week, depending on the metric). Full responsibility for creating and delivering the annual strategic plan ("Strat Plan") and annual operating plan ("AOP") in a very collaborative, qualitative fashion.

  • Key lead to implement SAP at Club Penguin.

  • Key person responsible for getting the actuals tied down each month, maintaining monthly balance sheet reconciliations for every balance sheet account, and governing compliance to corporate accounting policy as well as U.S. GAAP.

  • Key business support contact to Senior Management at Club Penguin and WDIG.

  • Very hands on finance and accounting manager who is willing to “role up his/her sleeves”, dig into the details, deliver results as a “sole contributor” at times, and do whatever it takes to get the job done.

Qualifications:

  • B.S. in Accounting or Finance
  • Minimum 12+ years of experience
  • Able to work effectively in a “dotted line” reporting structure
  • Experience helping to build a group or division from early stage to a group with a strong
    international presence
  • Strong analytical skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Previous experience in the interactive game or online media industries a plus (working
    with payment processors, industry standards, terminology, etc.)
  • SAP experience preferred
  • Strong operational accounting experience required
  • Strong FP&A experience required
  • CPA or accounting designation equivalent strongly preferred
  • Fully conversant with U.S. GAAP

Skills and Abilities:

  • Proven management skills
  • Proven presentation skills
  • Ability to “hold his/her own” in a Senior Management setting
  • Strong Excel skills
  • Solid analytical skills
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills
  • Detail oriented
  • Able to operate calmly and smoothly with rigor and discipline
  • Able to adapt, evolve and grow with the business
  • Flexible
  • Ability to develop and implement structured solutions for unstructured problems

Salary:

  • Competitive wage and benefits based on experience and qualifications

Location:

  • Must be able to work in Kelowna, BC, Canada
  • Club Penguin will assist with relocation
