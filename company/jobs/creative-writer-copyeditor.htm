Creative Writer/Copyeditor

Bring fun, kid-friendly approach to written content with a strong affinity for copyediting.

Responsibilities:

  • Proof all written content for consistency, accuracy and structure.
  • Work with a variety of creative animators, illustrators and designers to capture in words the essence of Club Penguin by providing weekly content appropriate to age demographic, project and medium.
  • Provide content to various departments on a project-by-project basis.
  • Provide creative contribution for narratives in a non-traditional storytelling platform.

 Qualifications:

Education:

  • Communications, Liberal Arts, Fine Arts, Journalism
Experience:
  • Background in Communications, Journalism, or creative writing. Experience with communicating to kids between 6-14.
Software:
  • Can competently navigate a Mac system

Skills and Abilities:

  • Proven skill in writing for the Web
  • Passionate about communicating to children
  • Skilled written and verbal communicator; strong interpersonal communications skills
  • Self-managing approach to projects
  • Proven grasp of syntax and proficiency in copyediting; detail oriented
  • Well developed sense of language; competently apply a ‘less is more’ philosophy to writing
  • Ability to meet fixed deadlines on a weekly basis; can juggle multiple tasks and projects simultaneously
  • Excels in collaborative environment and makes creative contributions on a variety of projects

Salary:

  • Competitive wage and benefits based on experience and qualifications.

Location:

  • Must be able to work in Kelowna, BC, Canada
