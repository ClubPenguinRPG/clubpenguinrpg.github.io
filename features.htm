Club Penguin Features

What do Free Accounts Get?

  • You get to choose and name your penguin
  • You get to chat with other Penguins
  • You get unlimited time in Club Penguin
  • You get to play most games
  • You get to earn coins (in case you want to become a member later)
  • You get to visit your buddies igloos
  • You get to be agents
  • You get to have fun!

What do Member Accounts Get? (including all the above items)

  • You get your own igloo
  • You get to purchase clothing
  • You get to buy furniture for your igloo
  • You get to buy items for future games
  • You get access to all of Club Penguin
