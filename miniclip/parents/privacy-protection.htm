Club Penguin is committed to providing a fun, entertaining, and safe website for people of all ages.

We are dedicated to safeguarding any personal information collected online and we ensure that our privacy policy and our information practices adhere to the U.S. Department of Commerce's Safe Harbor Principles and the Canadian Code of Practice.

We take special measures to help children protect their privacy while online. For example, we do not ask children to disclose more personal information than is necessary for them to participate in a particular activity, and we take efforts to prevent children from posting contact information.

 

 

1. The Information We Collect

The only personally identifiable information we collect from users 12 years old and younger is a parents valid email address, which is used to send a single message for Club Penguin account activation Read More.

2. How We Use the Information

The information we collect will only be used to improve the design and content of our site, and to occasionally offer relevant products, programs, and services.

We will never disclose the information we collect, except when required to do so by law, for example, in response to a court order or a subpoena. Read More.

3. Collection of Information for Third-Party Sites and Sponsors

We do not allow third-party companies to solicit or advertise to our users.

4. Cookies

Club Penguin uses "cookies" only to aid in improving web site design and content. Read More.

5. Security

We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data security, and correctly use the information we collect online.

6. Financial Information

The purchase of memberships requires additional information. That information is stored only by our financial institution and never used for any other purpose Read More.

7. Parental Review of Information

To protect your child's privacy and security, we will take reasonable steps to help verify your identity before granting you access to the personal information that we collect and maintain about your child Read More.

8. Contact Us

Contact the Privacy Department should you have any inquiries regarding the privacy policy and/or practices.
