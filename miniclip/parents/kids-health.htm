Kid's Health and Learning

Get out there and PLAY!

Penguins are a lot like kids. They’re happiest when they play!

Club Penguin is a great place for kids to have fun online, and while we are happy to provide this safe environment, it is not intended to replace fun physical activities kids have always enjoyed. Whether it’s joining a sports team, or playing an energetic game of tag or hide and seek, we encourage kids to engage in healthy, confidence-building play.
