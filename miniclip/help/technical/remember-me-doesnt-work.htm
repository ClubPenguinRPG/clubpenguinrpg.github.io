Remember me doesn’t work?

This happens when your flash player doesn’t have enough room in it to save information from Club Penguin. You can change your settings so that your flash player will have enough room to remember your penguin from now on. Just follow these simple steps.

  • If you have a PC
  • If you have a Mac

If you have a PC:

 

  1. Go to the Club Penguin website. Click on “Play Now!” Then click on “Start.” You are now on the Log In page.

  2. Right-click your mouse. A box will pop up. Click on the word “Settings.”

  3. A new box will pop up. On the bottom of this box you will see an icon that looks like a file folder. Click on the file folder icon.

  4. You will see a question: “How much information can clubpenguin.com store on your computer?” Underneath the question, you will see a bar. Use your mouse to move the arrow on the bar to the right. Move the bar until the number on the right reads “10 KB ” or more. That’s about the same memory you need to view a medium-sized thumbnail photo.

  5. Now you should have enough storage for the computer to remember your penguin each time you log in. Log in now, and click the box next to “Remember Me.” The next time you log in, you will see a picture of your penguin on the screen on the log in page.

If you have a Mac:

 

  1. Go to the Club Penguin website. Click on “Play Now!” Then click on “Start.” You are now on the Log In page.

  2. Hold down the “control” button on your keyboard and click the mouse at the same time. A box will pop up. Click on the word “Settings.”

  3. A new box will pop up. On the bottom of this box you will see an icon that looks like a file folder. Click on the file folder icon.

  4. You will see a question: “How much information can clubpenguin.com store on your computer?” Underneath the question, you will see a bar. Use your mouse to move the arrow on the bar to the right. Move the bar until the number on the right of the bar reads “10 KB” or more. That’s about the same amount of memory you need to view a medium-sized thumbnail photo.

  5. Now you should have enough storage for the computer to remember your penguin each time you log in. Log in now, and click the box next to “Remember Me.” The next time you log in, you will see a picture of your penguin on the screen on the log in page.
