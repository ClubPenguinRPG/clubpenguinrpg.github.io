How do I cancel my membership?

Membership cancellations only apply to monthly memberships purchased using a credit card. These memberships automatically recur each month to ensure uninterrupted membership service.

You may cancel your membership at any time. You will still receive the full membership period that you have purchased.

Canceling a membership requires the penguin name and password. If you don't know your password, please visit Forgot Password to have it emailed to you.

To cancel your account:

  • Go to edit your account, enter your penguin name and password, then select “Cancel your membership”.
  • Go to clubpenguin.com, click on the "Membership" tab, and select the option to cancel.
