Become a Member

While anyone can play Club Penguin for free, members have access to special features in Club Penguin. To find out more about a Club Penguin membership visit our Membership page.

Edit Your Account

To change your password, chat mode or cancel your membership while you are logged in to Club Penguin, click the icon on your player card menu. To cancel your monthly membership, the account username and password are required.

To change the chat mode, check the box at the bottom of the Edit Your Account screen.To learn more about each chat mode, go to Communicating.
