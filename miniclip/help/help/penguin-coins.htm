Penguin Coins

Play games to earn coins in Club Penguin. Use coins to purchase virtual items such as clothes, furniture, puffles and more. Coins are removed from the inventory when items are purchased.

Real money cannot be used to purchase coins, nor can coins be transferred from one account to another account.
