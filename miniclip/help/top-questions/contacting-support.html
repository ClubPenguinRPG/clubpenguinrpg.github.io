Contacting Club Penguin Support

Club Penguin Support is happy to answer your questions. To help us respond with the best information, keep these tips in mind regarding your password, email account and banning issues.
 
Make sure you contact us with the email address used to create your penguin.
 
Include proof of purchase for membership accounts. This includes:
 
The transaction ID number or order ID number located on your membership email.
 
If you cannot provide the transaction ID or order ID, please provide one of the following:

For credit card purchase:
the name on the credit card and the email address entered at the time of purchase.

For PayPal purchase:
the email address associated with the PayPal account, and the date it was purchased.

For Pay by Cash purchase:
the email address associated with your penguin account and the mailing address associated with your Pay by Cash account.

For SMS purchase:
the phone number used to purchase the account.

NOTE: We are unable to change the email address on an account without a membership.
