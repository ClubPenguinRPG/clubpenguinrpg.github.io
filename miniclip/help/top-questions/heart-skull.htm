What happened to the heart and skull emotes?

Club Penguin is a place for penguins to meet and have fun with buddies. We have received feedback from kids and parents telling us they didn’t like the heart and skull emotes for a variety of reasons. Because of this, we decided to replace them with brand new emotes. We hope the new emotes we created make communicating with your friends fun and easy. If you have ideas for more emotes, let us know. Send us some fan mail.
