Will there be any specific religious decorations or items during the holiday season in Club Penguin?

Club Penguin celebrates the holiday season with a variety of different festive decorations and items.  We acknowledge that this season has religious significance for many people; however, the festive focus in Club Penguin is designed to celebrate the spirit of the season and not to emphasize one or more religions above another. We wish everyone a Merry Christmas, Happy Holiday and Season’s Greetings! May it be full of fun and laughter.
