Why did you bring back the heart emote?

We have had quite a few people email us about this change.  Billybob (one of the founding penguins) recently wrote about this very thing in the developers blog.  Here's what he had to say:

"Sometimes, like everyone does, we make a mistake.  A few weeks ago we took away the heart and the skull emoticon because many of you had shared how they were being misused.  Once we removed them, we heard from even more of you how you were using the heart in great ways, such as showing your puffle how much you care for it or showing how much you loved the Coins for Change project.  In the end, we decided that we don’t want to punish all of you for something that only a few people were doing wrong.  So, we’ve decided to bring back the heart!"

We have decided though not to bring back the skull emote because Club Penguin is a positive place and we want it to remain that way.  There are plenty of other ways to say you don't like something.”  I'm sure you understand this.

Have fun using the heart in all sorts of cool ways."
