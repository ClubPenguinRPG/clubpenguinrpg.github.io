How do I cancel my membership and how do I know if it has been canceled?

Membership cancellations only apply to monthly memberships purchased using a credit card. These memberships automatically recur each month to ensure uninterrupted membership service.

You may cancel your membership at any time. You will still receive the full membership period that you have purchased.

Canceling a membership requires the penguin name and password. If you don't know your password, please visit Change my Password to have it emailed to you.

To cancel your membership:

Go to Cancel Membership enter your penguin name and password.  

You can confirm membership cancellation by entering your penguin name and password on the Edit Your Account page again.  You will see a message explaining the status of your membership.

  • If your membership is still actively being billed, the message will state: "Your membership is automatically renewed every month."
  • If you membership has been canceled but not expired, the message will state: "Your membership will expire on (and include your expiry date)."
  • If your membership has been canceled and expired, the message will state: "Your membership has expired."
