How do I purchase a membership for one month only?

Memberships purchased with a credit card are set to automatically renew each month.  If you would like to purchase a membership for one month only, you have a few options.  If you are using a credit card, simply cancel your membership after the initial purchase to avoid future billings.  Cancellations can be made at any time within the payment cycle. To cancel, please visit our Cancel Membership page.

Or, if you wish to purchase a membership for one month only, choose the PayPal, SMS or PayByCash payment options.  All of these options will apply for one month and do not automatically renew.
