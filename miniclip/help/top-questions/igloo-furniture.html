What is wrong with my igloo’s furniture? And, why doesn’t my igloo always load?

We are really excited to announce a new way of sorting furniture in your igloos. This will make finding furniture and decorating igloos easier.

We are aware there are loading delays with this new system and we appreciate you for letting us know about this. Due to the size of this update, you may need to wait a few extra moments as it loads. We appreciate your patience as we try to make it faster over the next few days.

When an update is made it is always a good idea to clear your computer’s cache, as this may speed up the loading time and allow you to access your igloo.

Simply click on the link below and select the web browser you are currently using for detailed directions:

http://www.clubpenguin.com/help/technical/clear-my-cache.htm

After successfully completing the steps above for the web browser please restart your computer before attempting to access your igloo on Club Penguin.
