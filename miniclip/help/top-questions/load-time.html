Why is it taking so long to load Club Penguin?

We are very excited that so many kids are playing on Club Penguin during the holidays.  Club Penguin may take a little longer than normal to load as there are more kids are joining and everyone is very excited about Coins for Change.  Here are a few tips so you can have the best online experience:

  • Try going on servers with the least amount of happy faces.  These servers will have the least amount of penguins, and will hopefully mean you have a faster connection.
  • Play on Club Penguin during non-peak hours.  
    Club Penguin is the busiest between
    3:00pm EST – 8:00pm EST.  If you go on Club
    Penguin before or after these times you may
    have faster load times.
  • Once on a server, stay there.  Switching back
    and forth between servers will only lessen the
    time you are actually playing as you will have to re-load.

Be patient this holiday season as many kids will be joining you in the holiday festivities online.
