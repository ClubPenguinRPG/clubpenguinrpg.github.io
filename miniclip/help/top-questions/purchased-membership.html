Club Penguin Help > I just purchased a membership but my account is not showing as a member.

I just purchased a membership but my account is not showing as a member.

Your membership will take effect as soon as we receive payment. Depending on the method of payment you chose, we may not have received your payment yet.

Credit Card: Instantaneous.
PayPal: If your PayPal account charges directly to a credit card, memberships are activated within 1 day.  If your PayPal account withdraws money directly from your bank account (eCheck) it may take up to 10 business days for the membership to be activated.
Mail: Purchases made by mail may take up to 4 weeks to process.

If the expected wait time listed above has passed, please log off and log back on again to activate your membership. If you still don't have membership status, please contact us and we will be happy to help.
