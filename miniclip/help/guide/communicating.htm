Ways to Communicate

  • You can talk to other penguins by chatting, using emotes, and doing actions.
  • To chat, if your account settings are set to Standard Safe Chat, you can either type in the text box at the bottom of the screen and press enter or click ‘send’; or, you can use the menu on the left.
  • If your penguin does not have a text box to type in messages, you are set to Ultimate Safe Chat.
  • In Ultimate Safe Chat, you cannot type in messages, but can communicate using the Ultimate Safe Chat menu on the left, as well as by emotes, actions, and greeting cards.
  • Note: If you are in Ultimate Safe Chat mode, you will only see other Ultimate Safe Chat messages, as well as actions, emotes, and greeting cards.
  • You can easily Change Your Safe Chat Level.

How to Send a Post Card

  • To send a post card to another penguin, you first need to open their player card.
    • If they’re in the same room as you, click on their penguin.
    • Or, you can click on your buddies list (Smiley Face icon, third from the right on your bottom menu), and click on their name.
  • Once you have their player card open, click the envelope icon on their card to “send mail”.
  • This will open the Post Card Catalog.
  • Pick the card you want to send (they cost 10 coins), and click “Yes” to send the card.

Breaking the Rules

  • If you swear or say something against the rules, you will be banned. This means you will not be able to use your account for a period of time – anywhere from 24 hours to forever.
  • If you are a member and you are banned, you will not be reimbursed for the time that you were banned.
  • A ban can happen automatically, which takes effect instantly, or be assigned by a moderator, which may take a little more time.

How a Penguin is Banned

A penguin can be banned either automatically or by a moderator.

  • An Automatic Ban occurs instantly when the player has said one of several flagged bad words.
  • A Moderator Ban happens when:
    • A player reports another player;
    • A record of all conversation at that time in that room is sent to the moderators, including filtered messages;
    • A moderator reviews the conversation;
    • If the moderator sees bad language or inappropriate behavior, they will ban the guilty penguin 24 hours for a first-time offense or 72 hours for a second or third offense.
    • Four or more offenses may result in a permanent ban.

Emotes

  • You can communicate by using the emotes and actions. Emotes, or emoticons, are small icons displaying a thought or emotion. Your emotes button is on the left of the toolbar. You can also display emotes by using Quick Keys.

Actions

  • Your penguin can do certain actions – wave, dance, sit, and throw a snowball. To do one of the actions, click on the actions icon – the sitting penguin (on the left side of your bottom menu). Then click on the action you want to do (sit, wave, or dance).
  • If you want to sit down, click the direction you want to face in the action menu.
  • You can also use Quick Keys to do actions.

Snowballs:

  • Another action you can do is throw a snowball.
  • To throw a snowball, click the Snowball icon (on the left of the toolbar), and then click a target.
