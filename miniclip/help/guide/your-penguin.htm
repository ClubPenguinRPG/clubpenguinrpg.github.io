Your Player Card

  • Click on your penguin to bring up your player card. Your card will tell you how many coins you have.
  • The top tab on the right is for clothing and accessories. The bottom tab is for options like colors, flags and backgrounds.
  • If you don’t own any items, you won’t see any listed. If you are a member, you can buy items with the coins you earn.

Buying Clothes (Members Only)

  • If you are a member, you can spend your coins to buy items or furniture for your penguin.
  • To buy clothing and accessories, go into the Gift Shop or Sport Shop and click on the catalog. Click ‘Buy Now’ in the catalog for the item you wish to purchase. When you buy an item, it gets added to your inventory shown on the player card.
  • Rare Items: these can be purchased on the Pirate Ship (The Migrator). It arrives in Club Penguin every two months and stays for up to two weeks. To purchase an item, click the catalog on the right of the screen. Rare items include clothing and furniture.
  • Your penguin will not automatically wear the item you purchased. You must edit (or customize) your penguin to wear your new clothing.

Penguin Clothes: Limited Editions

  • The clothing catalog for your penguin changes every month. With each new catalog, some items will no longer be available, and new items will be introduced.
  • If you see another penguin with an item that is not in the catalog, it is an older item.
  • Some clothing items remain in the catalog even though you can’t see them. They are hidden. To find these items use your mouse and watch for an icon to appear. Click on it to see the clothing item.
  • Sometimes, a limited-edition item will come back, so if you wait long enough you may have a chance to own it, too.

Customizing Your Penguin (Members Only)

  • To dress your penguin or change colors, click on your penguin or on the Edit button on the toolbar. This will bring up your player card.
  • Click on one of the tabs on the right of the card. This will show you all of the items you currently own (if any).
  • Click an item in the menu to put it on. To take an item off, click the item on your penguin, or click a new item to replace it.
  • The top tab is for clothing and accessory items; the bottom tab is for colors, flags and collectible pins.
  • To change your penguin’s color, select the lower tab of your player card. Click the color you want, and your penguin will change color. Flags and collectible pins are selected in the same way. Flags and pins, when selected will appear on the upper left side of your player card. You can wear one flag and one pin at a time.
  • If you don’t own any items, you won’t see any listed. Members can buy items at the Gift Shop with the coins they earn.
