Submit Fan Art

If you would like to send us your art, please follow these simple steps:

  • Draw it with your computer, or draw it on paper and scan it. Important: Make sure that the words are easy to read!
  • Save it as a .jpeg, .gif or .png. Important: We will not accept any other format.
  • Before submitting materials to Club Penguin, please see our Terms and Conditions Section #10.
  • Please attach your artwork file to an email and address it to

Our Fan Art mailbag is always full, so we might not be able to show your art. Please don’t be sad if yours doesn't get picked, we will try our very best!

Increase your chances by following the steps above very carefully.
