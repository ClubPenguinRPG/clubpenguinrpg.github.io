Wallpaper Instructions

To install a background on a PC:

  • Click 1280x1024 (standard size)
    or 1280x768 (wide screen)
  • Right click on the image
  • Select ‘Save As Wallpaper’

To install a background on a Mac:

  • Click 1280x1024 (standard size)
    or 1280x768 (wide screen)
  • Right click on the image
  • Select ‘Save Image’
  • Open System Preferences
  • Select ‘Desktop & Screensaver’
  • Choose Folder or Browse to find the image
