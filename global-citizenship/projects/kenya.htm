Kenya

Kenya lies across the equator in East Africa.

  • Your desire to protect the environment supported construction of a tree nursery to replant deforested areas, and school programs to teach kids the importance of tree planting. You’re also helping protect elephants with special educational programs for children and communities.
  • Thanks in part to support for children’s fundraising efforts provided through our Kids Matching Gift Program, hundreds of poor families now have access to classrooms, clean water supplies, alternative income programs and health care programs. To learn more about the program and how you can become involved go to: http://www.clubpenguin.com/global-citizenship/get-involved.htm
  • A new health clinic in rural Kenya means more than 30,000 children and families can get the medical care they need.

Adopt a Village

2008

Your enthusiasm and participation in Coins for Change 2007 supports a small rural Kipsigi village in Kenya (with just 256 families and 911 kids) to improve school for their children, gain access to health care and helps mothers learn new ways to earn money so their kids don't have to work. Funding supported:

  • 12 new classrooms
  • A clean water supply at the school
  • A library and community centre
  • Teacher accommodations
  • 6 latrines
  • A kitchen and cafeteria
  • 2 community gardens with fencing
  • A lunch program for kids
  • Sports equipment
  • Training for teachers
  • Health kits that help stop the spread of disease.

Funded by the New Horizon Foundation.

Adopt a Village

2008

A Masai village in Kenya received funding to:

  • Build eight new classrooms at their school
  • Refurbish the school library and office
  • Install a clean water source at the school
  • Train 40 student leaders over three years to help improve community health

Funding also supported a community mobile health clinic that serves more than 2,000 people, and alternative income programs for mothers so their children could attend school rather than going to work.

Funded by the New Horizon Foundation.
