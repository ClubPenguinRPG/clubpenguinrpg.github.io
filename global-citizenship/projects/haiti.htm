Haiti

Haiti occupies a third of the Caribbean island of Hispaniola.

Thanks to the kids who play Club Penguin, children affected by the earthquake in Haiti have a new school, complete with a playground and interactive mural. Our players sent in their drawings and voted for their favourites. Then a Club Penguin artist travelled to Haiti to paint the top choice on the wall of the new school.

  • Thousands of children and families can now access health care when they need it thanks to the completion of a solar panel project, which ensures Cerca La Source Medical Centre has electricity 24 hours a day.
  • Thanks in part to support for children’s fundraising efforts provided through our Kids Matching Gift Program, hundreds of poor families now have access to classrooms, clean water supplies, alternative income programs and health care programs. To learn more about the program and how you can become involved go to: http://www.clubpenguin.com/global-citizenship/get-involved.htm
  • Your passion for change also helped support a nutrition program and critical health care for malnourished and at-risk children.

Health Care

2009

Because you supported Coins for Change, kids in Haiti received the medical attention they needed. A hospital that cares for more than two million patients a year in one of the poorest regions of Haiti – the Central Plateau – got funding for 10 new nurses, a doctor, and nurses aids.

Funded by the New Horizon Foundation.
