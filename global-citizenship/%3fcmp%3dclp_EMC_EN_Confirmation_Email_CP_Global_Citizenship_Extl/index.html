OVERVIEW

What is Global Citizenship?

At Club Penguin, we believe everyone deserves a place to live, good health, an education and hope for a bright future.

Global Citizenship means giving back, and Club Penguin is committed to helping improve the lives of children and families around the world.

One way Club Penguin helps is by donating a portion of our net proceeds to worldwide causes. We also work hard to help the kids who play Club Penguin to understand and believe their actions can make a difference.

TEAMS

Teams in Action

Around the world, Club Penguin employees are making a difference in their communities by giving back and getting involved. Our charitable giving and volunteer matching program celebrates the time and energy that members of our team put into making the world a better place.

From planting trees to taking impoverished kids to the movies, along with in-house green efforts like recycling and carpooling, we try to do our part to give back to the community and protect the environment.

Rally for Change!

Every December, kids who play Club Penguin donate virtual coins and help change the world!
