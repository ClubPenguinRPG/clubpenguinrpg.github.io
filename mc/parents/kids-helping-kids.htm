Kids Helping Kids

At Club Penguin, we’re committed to improving the lives of children everywhere by supporting organizations doing important work on behalf of the world’s youngest and most vulnerable citizens.

A portion of the proceeds from every Club Penguin membership supports specific local and global projects. Currently, our funds are focused on two building projects in a small Ugandan village and a children’s hospital project in Romania.

Supporting these projects is our way of allowing the kids who join Club Penguin to play a role in securing a happier and healthier future for other children around the globe. It’s kids helping kids, something we can all be proud of.

Current Projects:

 

  • Uganda
  • Romania
We are currently organizing a non-profit foundation to manage the specifics of each project we undertake. This will allow us to provide you with more detailed, timely updates on projects and the difference these projects are making in many kids’ lives.
