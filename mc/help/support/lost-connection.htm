Lost connection?

This problem is normally caused by either playing Club Penguin from a slow computer, or by a poor quality Internet connection between your computer and our server. Even if you're using a super-fast computer connected using an ultra fast Internet connection it's still possible that one of the machines on the route from us to you isn't up to the task, and so you might get poor performance sometimes.

To reduce the problem you can try:

  • Close any unnecessary software. Don't try playing Club Penguin while
    downloading files or swapping music, this will slow your connection.
  • Try playing at a different time of day when the Internet is quieter and the
    various routers across the Internet aren't overloaded.
  • Try playing from a faster computer.
  • Try playing from a different ISP (Internet Service Provider)

Attention Wireless Internet Users

  • Please check that you have a "Very Good", or "Excellent" Internet connection.
    A poor wireless connection will cause you to lose connection.
