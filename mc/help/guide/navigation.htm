Walking Around

  • To walk around, click where you want to go, and your penguin will walk towards that spot.
  • If your penguin does not move where you want it to go, there may be something in your way, like a piece of furniture or a snow bank. Try clicking in a different direction to move around the obstacle.
  • To move through a door or to enter a room, click on the door.

Map

  • The Map is your shortcut to the rest of the penguin world.
  • Click the map icon on the bottom left-hand corner of the screen to open it. Once open, you can click on the area of Club Penguin that you want to go to next, and you will automatically go there.
