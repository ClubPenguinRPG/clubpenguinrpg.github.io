Secret Agents

  • Becoming a Secret Agent
  • Missions

Becoming a Secret Agent is a big responsibility!

  • As an agent, an important part of your job is to keep Club Penguin safe. One way you do this is by reporting bad penguins to a moderator.
  • To apply for the secret agent program, click the ‘M’ on the top right corner of your screen. Then, click the “Become a Secret Agent” button. Next, read ‘Your Mission’ carefully, and follow the application instructions.
  • Not everyone who applies will be accepted. If you are accepted, you will receive a spy phone in your inventory (scroll to bottom of inventory to locate).
  • You can use your spy phone to visit the HQ and other rooms in Club Penguin by clicking the phone icon in your inventory or player card.
  • To navigate with your phone, click on the left side scroll bar or on the spy phone screen to find the room you want to visit. Once the room you want to visit appears on the spy phone screen, click “Teleport” to go there. To visit the HQ, click the button “Visit HQ” and you will automatically be teleported to this secret location.

Missions

  • Agents are given special missions from time to time. They can be found in the HQ. To accept a mission, go to the HQ and click on the folder labeled “Top Secret” located at the bottom right screen.
  • To complete any mission, agents will be required to complete a series of tasks, solve problems and puzzles by using their spy tools, agent experience and knowledge of Club Penguin.
  • Agents who complete each mission will be rewarded. Rewards will vary depending on the nature of the mission.
  • Missions are periodically updated.
