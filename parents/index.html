Parents of Penguins

OVERVIEW

What parents should know

Welcome to Club Penguin, a virtual world for kids guided by an unwavering commitment to safety and creativity. In the form of a colorful penguin avatar your child can join the community, engage in a variety of fun activities, chat and play games with friends.

With its continually-evolving content, filtered chat and live moderators, Club Penguin offers fun for kids and peace of mind for parents. There’s no third-party advertising and it's free to play, but access to special features requires a membership.

PLAYER SAFETY

At Club Penguin, everything we do stems from our commitment to safe fun. We believe parental involvement is critical in keeping children safe online and we work in partnership with parents to support them in their efforts by providing safety measures that include sophisticated chat filters and on-site staff (moderators) who monitor online activity and chat.

Players are encouraged to follow the Club Penguin rules and can apply to become Secret Agents and participate in our version of a neighborhood watch program.

MEMBERSHIP

Membership supports Club Penguin's ability to maintain its proven commitment to creating a safe and fun online entertainment experience with no third-party advertising.

Club Penguin is free to play but membership provides more exploration and creativity, as well as access to special features, more games and exclusive activities. Every week there’s something new for members to discover and play.

GLOBAL CITIZENSHIP

At Club Penguin, we’re passionate about community engagement, and work hard to nurture a culture of giving. We value social involvement, and encourage our players to support and get involved in projects helping children and families around the world.

Go to Global Citizenship

Through events like Coins For Change, our players have the opportunity to rally together and make an impact. Our global community is working to help provide health care and build safe places for kids around the world, and protect the environment for future generations.
