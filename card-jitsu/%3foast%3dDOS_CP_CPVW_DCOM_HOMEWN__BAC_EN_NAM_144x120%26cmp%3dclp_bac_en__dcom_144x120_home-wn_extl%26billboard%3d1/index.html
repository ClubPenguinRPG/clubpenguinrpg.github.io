Master the ancient art of Card-Jitsu to become a Ninja.

How to become a Ninja

  • Go to the Dojo
  • Play Card-Jitsu to earn your belts
  • Challenge Sensei for your Ninja Mask

Become a Fire Ninja

  1. Play Card-Jitsu to become a ninja.
  2. Member ninjas, enter the Fire Dojo from the Hideout.
  3. Play Card-Jitsu Fire to earn your Fire Suit.
  4. Challenge Sensei.

Become a Water Ninja

  1. Play Card-Jitsu to become a ninja.
  2. Member ninjas, enter the Water Dojo from the Hideout.
  3. Play Card-Jitsu Water to earn your Water Suit.
  4. Challenge Sensei.

Trading Cards

  • Use Card-Jitsu cards to give yourself an edge in combat.
