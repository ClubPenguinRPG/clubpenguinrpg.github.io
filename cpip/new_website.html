WHAT IT'S ALL ABOUT:

While we've all been busy testing the new servers, there has been a lot of work started on a new look for the website, and it's all part of the Club Penguin Improvement Project.

The new look for the website launches on April 14. Check the CPIP blog for updates, and as always, your suggestions and feedback are very helpful.

A BIT OF BACKGROUND:

We've been listening to the ideas and suggestions that many of you have shared over the past several months about how to improve Club Penguin and they have been a huge source of inspiration for the new website design.

Many of you let us know that you'd like to see more art and an easier way to navigate around the site and that has been one of the main goals for the new design. After hearing from many of you that the new Club Penguin logo is better because it's more simple and clean, we wanted to continue in that direction with the rest of the site. Thanks for all your feedback about it!
