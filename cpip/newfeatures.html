COMING SOON:

For the past two years, we've been reading all of your ideas for improving Club Penguin and while the team was building new servers, they also got to work on some new features that many of you have been asking for. We may not have included all your great ideas yet, but with these new servers, we're hopefully going to be adding a lot more of them!

The team is working hard this month to make a few new features ready for the end of May and we wanted those of you who are helping with CPIP to be the first to hear about them and share your feedback.

Over the next few weeks, the CPIP blog will be updated each week with info about one of the new features planned for May. Your input will be a huge help as the team will be working hard on the features.

These new features are different than the content released on a regular basis in Club Penguin like new games, rooms, parties or catalogs. The new features will help with communicating with your buddies and will improve the interface of a couple different functions.

Read the [CPIP blog] for more info about each feature in the coming weeks.
